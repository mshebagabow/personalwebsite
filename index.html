About

I am a ojibway woman from a community called Long Lake #58 First Nation where I grew up in; Although I moved to Thunder Bay and Ginoogaming First Nation in between as a child. I am currently residing in Ginoogaming First Nation with my spouse Jeremiah Towigishig and my 4 beautiful children; Lawrence, Lewis, Meila, and Murphy.They are all awesome kids that hold their own unique personalities. My family enjoys spending their time outdoors, especially my husband Jeremiah and my oldest son Lawrence. They are love their have a passion forin trapping, snaring, and fishing. I have been a Teacher's Aide at Long Lake #58 First Nation since 2014 and soon to be registered with the Ontario College of Early Childhood Education. I'm a great person who is kind, loving, caring, and respectful. I care deeply about the care of my children and creating a stronger bond as a family. I'm an independeant person who holds her life values strong. I love spending time with my children and showing them the values in life such as showing respect to themselves and others and the importance of life, wildlife, and land; the value of love and caring towards themselves and towards others. My Hobbies

During the summers my family always enjoys spending time at the camp located just along the Long Lake; the only way we get to the camp is by boat and the kids love the boat ride. There we have a big fire and sit around and enjoy the evening roasting weiners and telling stories to our children and talking about our memories, these nights are filled with laughter and creating wonderful memories for our children to remember, so that they can tell to their children in the future. In the winter we usually go sliding right in out little community, and we also go for walks in the bush to check the traps that my husband sets, we also go on ski-doo rides. I strive to create happy and loving memories for our family.
